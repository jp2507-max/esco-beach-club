# iOS Store Assets Checklist

- Capture final iPhone screenshots from the shipped build, not simulator mock content.
- Show the strongest member-facing flows first: home/events, perks, member card, bookings, and profile benefits.
- Prepare at least one screenshot that explains the premium/private-membership value clearly without relying on placeholder text.
- Avoid showing internal staff-only QR tools unless they are necessary for review context.
- Keep legal/support URLs in metadata aligned with `store.config.json`.
- If you add localized App Store metadata later, localize screenshots and keywords together.
